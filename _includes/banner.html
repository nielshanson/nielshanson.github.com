While such data offers great promice of insight, capitalizing and extracting such data requires high-performance computing, distributed algorithms, and novel statistical and visualization techiques equiped to handle this data. My interests are to create high-performance algorithms and user-friendly software that aims to bring the end-user closer to the data and the story within it.
